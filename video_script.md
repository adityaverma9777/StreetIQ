# StreetIQ - Pitch Video Script (Extended Impact & Future Scope)

**Estimated Time:** ~3.5 minutes (Approx. 480 words)
**Tips for recording:** Speak clearly, take pauses between sections, and show the app doing exactly what you're describing on screen.

---

### [0:00 - 0:30] The Hook & Problem
**[Visual: You on camera, or a slide showing a broken road next to a standard map app]**
**You:** "Every day, millions face unpredictable and dangerous roads. Standard navigation apps give you directions, but they assume the roads are perfect. They don't warn you about deep potholes, severe cracks, or debris. This blind navigation leads to fatal accidents and severe vehicle damage. Today, I'm excited to show you **StreetIQ**—an AI-powered road intelligence platform that solves this."

### [0:30 - 1:00] Feature 1: Normal Navigation & Manual Reports
**[Visual: Screen recording of the app. Typing a destination and starting navigation. Then show the 'Report' tab]**
**You:** "At its core, StreetIQ provides smooth, turn-by-turn navigation by instantly calculating the most efficient path. But we also empower the community to map out hazards. Using our 'Report' tab, a user can safely snap a photo of a hazard, and our AI automatically pre-selects the hazard type based on the photo to file a manual report."

### [1:00 - 1:40] Feature 2: Activating the AI Scan & Auto Surveys
**[Visual: Tapping the "AI Scan" camera button in the center. Show the camera feed running]**
**You:** "But what if you are actively driving? That's where StreetIQ shines. This solution can be installed directly into modern cars or paired with existing dashcams. Once connected, it automatically records and reports all road conditions without the driver lifting a finger. 
We trained our very own YOLOv8 machine learning model from scratch specifically for complex Indian road conditions. And because the AI runs entirely inside your browser using TensorFlow.js, it scans the road in real-time right on the edge."

### [1:40 - 2:10] Feature 3: Smart Gating & Real-Time Sync
**[Visual: Point out the FPS counter and the "Scanning" / "Paused" indicator]**
**You:** "We built in 'Smart Motion Gating'. If your car stops at a red light, the AI automatically pauses scanning to save battery and prevent duplicate reports. 
When it spots a hazard, only that specific image frame and its GPS coordinates are sent to our database. Instantly, our Supabase backend broadcasts that hazard to the map of every other driver in the area."

### [2:10 - 2:50] The Impact & Benefits
**[Visual: Show the map populating with a new hazard pin. Then show a slide highlighting key benefits]**
**You:** "The impact here is massive:
1. **Saving Lives:** We tackle the root cause of accidents by letting drivers slow down or re-route before hitting a massive pothole.
2. **Empowering Citizens:** Anyone can now monitor their local infrastructure and hold local governments accountable without filing RTI requests.
3. **Highly Scalable:** Because our proprietary AI processes everything on the user's device, we don't need expensive server farms to process videos. The system scales sustainably for free."

### [2:50 - 3:30] Future Scope & Outro
**[Visual: Switch back to you on camera, or a closing slide with future roadmap]**
**You:** "Looking ahead, our future scope is highly ambitious:
- We plan to partner with dashcam manufacturers for **Direct Hardware Integration**.
- We want to build **Dedicated Municipal Dashboards** so city planners get a free, auto-updating heatmap of infrastructure to issue repair tenders faster.
- We'll expand detection to civic hazards like broken streetlights and missing manhole covers.
- And finally, we will introduce **Community Gamification** to reward drivers with fuel discounts for keeping the map updated.

With StreetIQ, we are passively turning every single driver into an automated road surveyor to keep our cities safe. Thank you."
